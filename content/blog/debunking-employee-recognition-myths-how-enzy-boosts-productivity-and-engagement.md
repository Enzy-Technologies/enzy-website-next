Some believe that employees will always give their best effort, whether or not they receive recognition. Yet **78% of workers report working harder and with more drive when their efforts are rewarded.** This is especially true in younger generations, making it increasingly more important for leaders to recognize their employees.

Just because gamification has come on strong recently does not mean it's going anywhere—**89% of employees believe gamification can boost workplace productivity.** By integrating rewards, challenges, and friendly competition, gamification taps into intrinsic motivation, making work more engaging and performance-driven.
