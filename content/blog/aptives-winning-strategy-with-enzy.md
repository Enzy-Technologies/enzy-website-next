### **Introduction**‍

Aptive, a pioneering force in the pest control industry, partnered with Enzy to implement competition-driven incentives designed to elevate sales performance. This case study delves into the measurable impact of these incentives on Aptive’s sales metrics and overall business growth.

### **Challenge**‍

Faced with the need to evaluate the effectiveness of its incentive programs, Enzy aimed to determine whether Aptive's strategic investment in Enzy's platform was delivering tangible improvements in sales outcomes. The complexity lay in analyzing over 150 competitions conducted in 2024 to extract meaningful insights.

### **Methodology**‍

The study utilized sales data from 2024, focusing on Serviced Contract Value and daily incentive participation. By employing robust modeling techniques, the analysis considered variables such as day of the week, sales regions, and geographic areas to assess the incentives' impact on sales.

### **Key Findings**

* **Significant Sales Growth:**
  + Enzy's incentives led to an average increase of $88 in daily sales per representative, translating to a 9.2% rise in daily serviced contract value.
  + Overall, Aptive experienced a 4.5% uplift in total summer sales for 2024.
* **Behavioral Patterns:**
  + Sales performance peaked early in the week, with Mondays showing the highest activity.
  + Geographic differences highlighted the necessity for region-specific incentive strategies to maximize sales effectiveness.
* **Data-Driven Insights:**
  + The linear regression model identified critical factors influencing sales, including the significant role of incentive participation.
  + The analysis revealed a non-linear relationship between the frequency of incentives and sales performance, emphasizing the value of strategic incentive timing.
* **Enhanced Employee Engagement:**
  + The structured incentives not only boosted sales but also fostered a high-performance culture among sales representatives, enhancing motivation and engagement.

### **Conclusion**‍

This case study illustrates the substantial benefits of using Enzy’s platform to manage and execute incentive-driven competitions. Aptive's experience demonstrates how targeted incentives can significantly enhance sales performance, delivering a measurable return on investment. With a 9.2% increase in sales, Enzy's approach proves to be a powerful tool in driving business success.
